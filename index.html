AIM OF THE GAME

Keep the televisions under control! There are three different types of TV which will require different strategies:

Eye: Click

Mouth: Hold

Hand: Hover

TIP: Mouths spread to neighbouring televisions if you don't deal with them fast enough!

TIP: Hands will randomly activate other tiles, meaning things can quickly spiral out of control if left unchecked!
